BBQ Chicken Salad

INGREDIENTS

  • Grilled Chicken
  • Cilantro-Lime Dressing
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Black Beans
  • Corn
  • Tomatoes
  • Red oninon
  • Avocado
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Tortilla Strips
  • BBQ Sauce

STEP BY STEPS

  1. Season and grill the chicken.
  2. Make the cilantro-lime salad dressing by pulsing the ingredients together in a food processor.
  3. Toss the lettuce with about half of the dressing.
  4. Top salad with black beans, corn, tomatoes, red onion, avocado, cheese and chicken.
  5. Drizzle salads with more of the cilantro-lime dressing.
  6. Top with tortilla strips and drizzle with BBQ sauce.
