Healthy Brocoli Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh broccoli, cut into bite size pieces.
  • One apple, chopped. Toss the apple with a little lemon juice to prevent browning
  • Fresh blueberries
  • Red onion, chopped
  • Salt
  • Sunflower seeds

STEP BY STEPS


Once you have your salad ingredients in the bowl, make the broccoli salad dressing. In a bowl, whisk together plain Greek yogurt, honey, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar and poppy seeds.
