Salmon Salad

Salmon Salad Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh Salmon Fillets
  • Olive Oil, Salt and Pepper
  • Baby Spinach
  • Red onion
  • Walnuts
  • Avocado
  • Fresh Blueberries
  • Goat Cheese
  • Honey Mustard Dressing

STEP BY STEPS

  1. Bake the salmon in the oven or pan sear it in a skillet on the stove. I find that baked salmon is the most foolproof. You simply rub the salmon with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and bake for about 12-18 minutes, until itâ€™s cooked through.
  2. While the salmon cooks, make the salad dressing. Whisk together all of the dressing ingredients in a small bowl: olive oil, honey, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, salt and pepper.
  3. To assemble the salads, divide the spinach between four large salad bowls. Top the spinach with the sliced red onion, toasted walnuts, sliced avocado, blueberries and crumbled goat cheese.
  4. Place a cooked salmon fillet on top of each salad. You can add the salmon to the salads when it is warm, or let it cool to room temperature. Drizzle the dressing over the salads and serve.
